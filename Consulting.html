Why Basic Consulting Training?

Available on-site or online in German, English or Japanese.

What you’ll learn:

  • Structured problem analysis
  • Systematic process improvement
  • Root cause identification
  • Creative solution development
  • Effective team facilitation

Your benefits:

  • Flexible format (online/in-person)
  • Compact duration (4–6h)
  • Multilingual: DE | EN | JP
  • Immediately applicable
